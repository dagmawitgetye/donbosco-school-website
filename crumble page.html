Don Bosco Mekanissa: A Sanctuary of Hope

In the heart of Addis Ababa, Don Bosco Mekanissa stands as more than just an educational institution; it is a beacon of transformation. Since its inception, our school has been dedicated to serving the youth of Ethiopia, providing a safe haven where academic excellence meets technical mastery and spiritual growth.


Our pedagogy is defined by the Preventive System—a unique blend of Reason, Religion, and Loving-Kindness. At Mekanissa, we believe that education is a matter of the heart. We don't just teach subjects; we walk alongside our students as mentors and friends.
