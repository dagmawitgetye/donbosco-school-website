About Our School

Don Bosco General School is committed to providing quality education that nurtures both the mind and character of students. Inspired by the values of Saint John Bosco, we focus on academic excellence, discipline, and moral development to ensure every child reaches their full potential.

Why Don Bosco?

Where reason lights the mind, and faith warms the heart, Where kindness guides each hand and learning is an art. At Don Bosco, we grow in knowledge, courage, and care, Preparing young souls for a world they’re ready to share.
