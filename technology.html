LABORATORY FACILITIES

Physics Laboratory

A center for high-level mechanical and electronic experimentation. Our physics lab is built to help students visualize the invisible forces of the universe.

Facility Highlights:

  • Digital Storage Oscilloscopes
  • Precision Laser Bench Sets
  • Advanced Kinematics Sensors

Bio-Physics & Biology

Explore life at its most fundamental level. Our biology department provides the tools necessary for cellular analysis and molecular research.

Microscopy & Analysis:

  • High-Resolution Compound Microscopes
  • Scanning Digital Microscopes
  • Incubation & Centrifuge Stations

Chemistry Hub

Where reaction meets innovation. This facility houses a massive inventory of reagents and modern tools for chemical synthesis.

Chemical Inventory:

  • 500+ Organic and Inorganic Reagents
  • Advanced Fume Hoods & Safety Systems
  • Digital Titration & Distillation Units

Modern Pedagogy

Our teaching methods focus on active learning. We bridge the gap between complex theory and hands-on application through STEM-focused instruction.

Learning Design:

  • Collaborative Problem-Solving Spaces
  • Digital Simulation Tools
  • Interactive Project-Based Curriculum
