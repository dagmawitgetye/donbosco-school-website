Our Senior School

Welcome to a space defined by academic ambition and personal integrity. In our Senior School, we recognize that the final years of high school are the most critical. This is why we specifically prepare our students for the rigor of Matric, providing them with the analytical tools and the resilience needed to excel in their final examinations.

Our comprehensive curriculum is built on a foundation of core excellence. Students engage deeply with Mathematics through advanced calculus, master the nuances of English literature, and explore the complexities of the Sciences through Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Our Humanities program further ensures a rich understanding of History and Geography.

Beyond the textbooks, our students enjoy learning with high-quality supplies—from modern laboratory kits to the latest digital resources. These tools ensure our seniors are ready for the world beyond our gates.
