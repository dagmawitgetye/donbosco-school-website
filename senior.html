Welcome to Don Bosco Senior School

Excellence, Innovation, and Character Development – Preparing Students for National and Global Success.

About Our Senior School

Don Bosco Senior School in Mekanisa is committed to nurturing young adults into confident, ethical, and academically excellent individuals. Our curriculum integrates rigorous academics with practical skills, innovation, and personal development.

Curriculum & Excellence

The senior school curriculum combines in-depth study of chemistry, biology, physics, mathematics, and English with arts, ICT, and extracurricular programs. In the 12th-grade matriculation exams, an impressive 99% of our students successfully graduate each year.

ICT & Innovation

Our computer labs are fully equipped with modern technology, high-speed internet, and coding platforms. Students learn software development and digital literacy to prepare them for technological careers.

Where Our Students Shine Globally

Our alumni have earned places at prestigious universities, pursued advanced studies in STEM, and now contribute significantly in leading organizations around the world. We also offer scholarships for outstanding students to study in Italy.
