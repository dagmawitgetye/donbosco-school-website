Our Senior School

Welcome to a space defined by academic ambition and personal integrity. In our Senior School, we recognize that the final years of high school are the most critical. This is why we specifically prepare our students for the rigor of Matric, providing them with the analytical tools and the resilience needed to excel in their final examinations.

Curriculum

Our comprehensive curriculum is built on a foundation of core excellence. Students engage deeply with Mathematics through advanced calculus and problem-solving, master the nuances of English literature and communication, and explore the complexities of the Sciences through hands-on Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Furthermore, our Humanities program allows for a rich understanding of History and Geography, creating a well-rounded educational experience.

Beyond the textbooks, our students enjoy learning with high-quality supplies they can truly rely on—from modern laboratory kits and artistic mediums to the latest digital resources. These tools, combined with our world-class environment, ensure our seniors are ready for the world.
