Registration Guide

Welcome to Our Community

We are excited to help you begin your journey. Please follow this comprehensive guide to ensure a smooth enrollment process for the upcoming academic year.

Phase 01

Secure Your Online Access ID

Before visiting the campus, you must register through our secure web portal. This system generates a unique **Admission ID Number**. This number is your "key" to the school; security will require this ID to grant you entry for your official appointment.

Phase 02

On-Campus Registration & Documents

Based on the appointment date provided by the website, you will visit the Don Bosco Registrar's office. **This is where formal enrollment begins.** You must bring your physical file, including all necessary paperwork.

Required Documentation Checklist:
  • Your Printed Online Admission ID Number
  • Official Academic Transcripts from your previous school
  • Birth Certificate and two passport-sized photographs
  • A valid government ID of the parent or guardian
Phase 03

The Summer Program Experience

To prepare for our rigorous academic standards, all prospective students for Grades 2 through 12 are required to attend our Summer Class. This program helps us evaluate your readiness and allows you to meet your future classmates.

*Important: Grade 1 applicants skip this phase and proceed directly to the assessment phase.

Phase 04

Entrance Exam & Final Acceptance

The final step in our process is the Entrance Examination. For Summer Class students, this takes place at the end of the session. For Grade 1, it is scheduled shortly after registration. Once you pass this exam, you are officially a Don Bosco student!
